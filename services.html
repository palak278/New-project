Our Services

Research Article Writing

We provide ghost writing services, where the author's name remains confidential. Our professional writers help authors craft high-quality research articles for publication in reputed journals. We act as an intermediary between researchers and expert writers to ensure well-structured, impactful, and publication-ready manuscripts.

Research Guidance & Consultation

Our team of experienced professionals offers comprehensive research guidance and support for journal publications. Many authors may not be fully aware of the research publication process, and we provide expert consultation, proofreading, and end-to-end support to ensure seamless submission.

Conference Proceedings Support

We assist researchers in compiling and publishing conference proceedings in reputed journals. From manuscript editing to formatting and indexing, we provide a structured approach to ensure successful conference publication.

Article Writing Assistance

Our expert ghostwriters and research professionals help in writing high-quality research papers. Whether you need content creation, structuring, or enhancements, we ensure that your manuscript meets the highest academic and research standards.

DOI Registration Services

A Digital Object Identifier (DOI) provides a unique and permanent reference for research articles. We offer DOI registration services to help scholars ensure long-term accessibility and easy identification of their work on platforms like CrossRef.

Open Journal Systems (OJS) Support

We provide Open Journal Systems (OJS) solutions, a widely used open-source platform for managing and publishing research articles. Our services include: ● Journal setup and website development ● Manuscript submission system ● Editorial workflow management ● Indexing and archiving

Web Development & Maintenance

Our expert web development team designs, maintains, and optimizes websites for researchers, institutions, and journals. We ensure: ● User-friendly front-end interfaces ● Secure back-end systems for data management ● Mobile-responsive designs

Digital Marketing & Research Promotion

At Blue Publication, we leverage digital marketing strategies to enhance visibility and reach. Our promotional services include: ● Social Media Marketing (SMM): Expanding research outreach through platforms like Facebook, LinkedIn, and Twitter. ● Search Engine Optimization (SEO): Optimizing research-related content for better online discoverability. ● Google & Facebook Ads: Paid promotions to target academic and research professionals. ● Email Marketing: Targeted outreach to scholarly authors and institutions.

Copy Editing & Formatting

We provide professional copy editing and formatting services, ensuring that manuscripts are free of errors and meet journal guidelines. Our expert editorial team ensures: ● Correct grammar, punctuation, and terminology usage ● Precise and professional structuring ● Clear and concise research presentation
